Battery Enclosure Compliance Framework

Synqron’s battery enclosures are designed with international and Australian standards in mind, ensuring grid safety, modular scalability, and thermal/fire protection readiness. The table below summarizes the major design steps and associated standards.

Design Step Applicable Standards Purpose / Scope
Battery Type & Safety IEC 62619, UL 1973 Safe lithium-ion cell integration in stationary systems
Enclosure Structure ISO 12944, IEEE 693, AS/NZS 1170 Corrosion resistance, seismic/wind resilience
Thermal & Fire Management UL 9540, NFPA 855, UL 9540A Fire suppression, runaway protection
Ingress Protection IEC 60529, ISO 12944 Dust and water protection for outdoor systems
Monitoring Systems IEC 60730, UL 1973 Control logic for thermal, voltage, and fault protection
Grid Integration IEEE 1547, IEC 62933-5-2 Ensures safe synchronization with power networks
Workplace Safety & QA ISO 9001, ISO 45001, ISO 14001 Quality, safety, and environmental certifications

This framework aligns with Synqron’s modular pilot-phase designs and is referenced in REZ-compliant deployments.
